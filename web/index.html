Personal learning loop

Every student gets guidance that follows their own progress.

1

Teachers provide the course path

Course bundles keep lectures, questions, review prompts, and version metadata together.

2

The tutor adjusts inside the path

Learn and review sessions use the student's answers, progress, and mistakes to choose the next step.

3

Students see progress and wins

Correct work, recovered mistakes, and completed review steps become visible progress instead of hidden effort.

For teachers

Publish once, personalize continuously.

Prepare course versions, upload validated bundles, approve enrollments, and review each student's progress and mistake patterns.

For students

Study with guidance that fits.

Browse the catalog, request enrollment, download approved bundles, and receive practice that follows your pace and weak spots.

For continuity

Turn mistakes into the next lesson.

Sync enrollment state, session history, progress, and mistake-book signals so review stays targeted.
