## What we collect

### Account & security
- Username (we prohibit using email/phone number as username)
- Email (used only for password recovery and security notices; not for marketing)
- Password hash (we do not store plaintext passwords)
- Account timestamps (created_at, last_login_at) and account status (e.g., active/suspended)

### Marketplace public profile (teacher-controlled)
- Display name, bio, course descriptions
- Optional contact details you choose to publish (voluntary)

### Encrypted content for multi-device sync (End-to-End Encrypted)
To support multi-device synchronization and device changes, we store **end-to-end encrypted** content (e.g., chat/messages and progress records). We **cannot read** this encrypted content.

### Limited plaintext metadata (for sync & delivery)
To operate synchronization and delivery, we store limited plaintext metadata such as:
- Message/event type (e.g., create/update/delete)
- IDs (course_id, conversation_id, event_id/message_id)
- Sender/recipient account identifiers (internal IDs), device identifier
- Timestamps and payload size
We do **not** use this metadata for profiling or advertising.

### Connection metadata (temporary)
To establish peer-to-peer connections (when available), we temporarily process necessary network connection information such as **IP/port** and **ICE candidates** during connection setup.
- We do **not** use it for profiling or advertising.
- We do **not** keep **persistent logs** of such connection metadata (transient processing only for connection setup).

## What we do NOT do
- We do not review or analyze the encrypted message/progress content.
- We do not sell personal data.

## Purposes
- Authentication and account security
- Operating the public course marketplace and enrollment approvals
- Multi-device synchronization (encrypted content) and delivery
- Establishing peer-to-peer connections (connection setup only)

## Retention
- Encrypted sync content + related plaintext metadata: retained on a rolling basis for up to **365 days**, unless deleted earlier by the user (or required for abuse/security handling).
- Delivery queue for offline transfer: items expire after **7 days** (undelivered items are removed).
- Connection metadata: transient during connection setup; not persisted.
- Backups: database is backed up daily and kept on a rolling basis for **30 days**.

## Deletion
- Users can delete content within the app. Deletions are **irreversible** and are synchronized to other devices and authorized recipient devices (e.g., teacher device) within the service.
- Users can delete their account in-app. Account deletion disables login immediately and triggers deletion of account data, marketplace profile/listings, and associated encrypted content, subject to backup rotation (30 days).
