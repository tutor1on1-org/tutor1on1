# Marketplace Policy (v2)

This policy applies to all teacher profiles, course listings, and related content published in the marketplace.

## 1. Teacher self-disclosure
Teachers may publish their own contact details **voluntarily**.

When publishing contact details, teachers must confirm:
- The contact details are shared voluntarily by the teacher
- The content does not include anyone else’s personal information
- The content may be removed and the account may be restricted/suspended if it violates this policy

## 2. Prohibited content
- Publishing anyone else’s personal information (doxxing)
- Harassment, hate, threats, or abusive content
- Illegal content or instructions encouraging illegal activity
- Impersonation or fraudulent claims
- Spam or unsolicited advertising

## 3. Reporting and enforcement
- Users can report marketplace content.
- We will review reports and may remove content and/or restrict accounts.
- We do not guarantee response time, but may take expedited action for clear privacy violations.
