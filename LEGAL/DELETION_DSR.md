# Account Deletion & Data Subject Requests (DSR) (v2)

**Contact:** support@YOUR_DOMAIN (replace)

## 1. Delete account (in-app)
You can delete your account in the app. After deletion:
- Your account becomes immediately inaccessible (login disabled).
- We delete your account record, email used for recovery, marketplace profile/listings, enrollments, and related service records, subject to backup rotation.

## 2. Retention summary
- Account, marketplace, enrollment, bundle, and operational records are retained as needed to operate the service and handle security or abuse issues.
- Backups: database backed up daily; rolling retention **30 days**.

## 3. Requests and response time
You may contact us for requests related to your account data. We aim to respond within **30 days**.
