# Account Deletion & Data Subject Requests (DSR) (v2)

**Contact:** support@YOUR_DOMAIN (replace)

## 1. Delete content (in-app)
You can delete messages/progress within the app.
- Deletions are **irreversible**
- Deletions are synchronized to your other devices and authorized recipient devices within the service

## 2. Delete account (in-app)
You can delete your account in the app. After deletion:
- Your account becomes immediately inaccessible (login disabled).
- We delete your account record, email used for recovery, marketplace profile/listings, and associated encrypted content/metadata, subject to backup rotation.

## 3. Retention summary
- Encrypted sync content + related plaintext metadata: rolling retention up to **365 days**, unless deleted earlier.
- Offline delivery queue: expires after **7 days**.
- Backups: database backed up daily; rolling retention **30 days**.

## 4. Requests and response time
You may contact us for requests related to your account data. We aim to respond within **30 days**.
